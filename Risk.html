Risks


In the process of completing the website, there will certainly be a lot of points that need to be noted and improved. The first is about AI's recognition capabilities. AI needs a lot of time to learn to recognize the content of all kinds of images. Because images are more diverse and overlapping than text, it is possible that the AI ​​will sometimes not recognize them correctly, making this feature ineffective. Next, when we create this website, we plan to host as many images as possible. This will cost a lot for the image store on the server. In addition, when the server is overloaded, the system may crash. In addition, when a lot of people log in or go to the website at the same time, it is very likely that there will be a lag due to data overflow. However, it is also a good thing that many people visit the site. On the contrary, after completion, there is also the possibility that we cannot reach users even though we may have spent a lot of money on marketing; It may be because there are similar websites on the market and the features of our website are not so outstanding.
