Member's Ideal Jobs Comparison


Similarities

It is widely accepted that no individuals are alike. However, I noticed some commonalities amongst the group members as a result of each individual's Ideal Job. The most notable thing we have in common is that the job we aspire to is inextricably linked to the area we are currently studying. In the case of me and Binh, we are both studying Digital Marketing, and our ideal career would also be related to marketing, particularly tied to a game firm. Similarly, Anh and Khang expect working in an IT-related workplace.


Differences

Since I and Duong are majoring in digital marketing, we are concentrating our efforts on finding jobs related to my course, as opposed to Khang and Anh, who are interested in the IT field.

Due to Duong's ambition to advance in a Wolffun firm, her employment requirements are more demanding than mine. For instance, she must have at least eight years of experience developing marketing strategies for products. On the other hand, my dream work would allow me to spend more time online. If I want to be considered for a position at Maplelab, I need to understand how to promote on social media and use SEO.

For Khang’s ideal job, it requires knowledge of HTML5 and at least one year of experience managing a team. Besides, Anh's dream work requires knowledge of some IT languages, such as Python or Caffe, but does not require time working experience. Moreover, due to Anh's love for artificial intelligence, he wishes to join the KMS company's AI development team. On the other hand, Khang is interested in web development so he decided to apply for this job in Ubisoft.
