Skills and jobs


IT developer

The IT developer that we are looking for should have sufficient knowledge regarding web development and maintenance. The candidates should have a Bachelor degree in Information Technology or related field. Ability to use Wix, Spring Boot, and Flask is a must. The main responsibility of the IT developer is fixing bugs in the system and implementing new minor features if required. Being familiar with Computer Vision is a plus to handle tasks related to image classification. It is necessary for the developer to have sufficient communicating skills to collaborate well with other people in the team. The developer should be willing to learn new things to enhance and update the system with the latest technologies. Moreover, the IT candidate will also be responsible for helping other non-IT members in the team if they have any IT-related issues.


Human Resource

The HR requirements are that they must have at least one year of expertise in the recruiting industry, from sourcing to training, as well as strong interview and candidate evaluation skills. Furthermore, because we may be working with foreigners, HR must communicate effectively in English to comprehend and assist consumers. Furthermore, In our company, the major responsibility of HR is to conduct interviews, with a marketing approach to drive talent acquisition activities. In addition, Good communication, networking, and negotiation skills are essential for HR because they will assist HR in understanding the strengths and weaknesses of the people they interview. Therefore, they can select the most suitable candidates, which can help develop the company. Besides, Our organization will provide opportunities for HR to grow, so they must be self-motivated and self-learning if they want to advance in their careers.


Customer service manager

Customer service and web management: without the presence and help of AI, the image review will be taken care of by the team's own human resources to ensure image quality. This person will also take care of the connection between the service and the customer, answering questions, solving problems that may affect the website. So first this person needs to be able to handle multitasking, because this not only increases the productivity of the team and the company but also saves money for the company while boosting output. Besides, has good problem solving and situation handling ability. Good communication in English is an advantage. Small requirements include knowing how to take photos, basic photo editing, as well as being creative and proactive at work

Accountant

The important requirements for an accountant are having professional qualifications and accounting skills. Next, being able to understand English is not only a necessary skill of every accountant but also a regulation introduced by the Law on Accounting of Vietnam, which requires that any individual who wants to practice must have a full degree and practicing certificate licensed by the state or authorized agency. Having good foreign language skills will help accountants understand in more detail the legal system, rules and standards of partners, helping the process of working, exchanging content, signing cooperation take place. favorable. Besides, honesty is also an important quality to have in an accountant. Accounting work is closely related to the company's revenue, that's why people in the profession want to stabilize and develop their careers, they need to build their credibility with their superiors. If at work, there are errors, mistakes in data and reports, an honest accountant will know how to honestly solve them, be ready to be disciplined and actively find solutions to overcome the problem. Often an accountant will have to be in charge of many different work items such as reporting, collecting documents, recording data and many other arising jobs. To meet the job in the best way, a professional accountant needs to have good analytical, synthesis and observation skills to promptly respond and offer appropriate solutions for the job.
