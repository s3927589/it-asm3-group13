Three test results of Pham Thi Thuy Duong
1. The Myers-Briggs Test

Figure 1. My Myers-Briggs test result

(Source: https://practicalpie.com/myers-briggs-type-indicator)

One may argue that the test accurately reflects my personality (introvert) and mental wellness. Regardless of whether I enjoy crowds or am comfortable attending large gatherings, I still struggle to acclimate to speaking and mingling with strangers in real life.


2. Learning Style Test

Figure 2. My learning style test result

(Source: http://www.educationplanner.org/students/self-assessments/learning-styles-quiz.shtml?event=results&A=2&V=12&T=6)

The test results do not fully describe my learning style's excessive dependence on one approach, yet Tactile has a minor advantage over the others. In retrospect, I frequently murmur silently while memorizing, get a sense of thinking faster and better while engaging in physical activities, and am easily bored and exhausted when engaging in activities or theoretical subjects such as language studies, analysis, and professional assessment.


3. The Big Five Test

Figure 4. My Big Five test result

(Source: https://www.123test.com/report/3KSOC8ZZCAPS2DTBSZ/)

To be honest, the test results were not what I expected. I am an open-minded individual who is constantly striving for innovation, as well as perfect precision and a strong sense of duty. The score is perplexing, particularly in the Agreeableness section. Although I'm not very calm, I do my best to resolve conflicts gently; I'm just really irritable around individuals I care about.
